Healthy weight


Move More, Eat Better is a national health awareness program of the Weight-control Information Network (WIN) that encourages black women ages 18 and older to reach and maintain a healthy weight by being more physically active and making healthier food choices.

Anyone who sees a need in his or her community and wants to help can start a Sisters Together program. This recently updated guide outlines six steps to help you start your program.
